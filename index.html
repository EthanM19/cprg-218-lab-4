Workout Plans

Basic Plan: Our Basic Plan offers access to our gym facilities, including a range of basic cardio equipment and a dedicated weightlifting area. Members also have access to locker rooms for their convenience. This plan is perfect for individuals looking for a simple yet effective workout routine. The Basic Plan is available at an affordable price of $30 per month.

Premium Plan: Upgrade to our Premium Plan for an enhanced fitness experience. In addition to all the features of the Basic Plan, members enjoy access to a variety of group fitness classes such as yoga, Zumba, and spin classes. They also receive personalized workout plans from our certified trainers and can relax in our sauna and steam rooms after their workouts. The Premium Plan is available for $50 per month.

Ultimate Plan: For the ultimate fitness experience, choose our Ultimate Plan. In addition to all the features of the Premium Plan, members receive exclusive access to one-on-one personal training sessions tailored to their fitness goals. They also benefit from nutrition consultation and personalized meal planning services. Our Ultimate Plan includes towel service and complimentary protein shakes to support members in achieving their fitness objectives. Experience the pinnacle of fitness with our Ultimate Plan, priced at $100 per month.
